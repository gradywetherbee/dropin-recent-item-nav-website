What is Jango?

Jango connects you to adventures at Northwestern that are happening today. Find workout buddies, join sports games, or grab drinks with new people.

How does it work?

Check out the map of nearby adventures that other NU students have posted. Find one that looks fun, join the whatsapp group via the link on the map, and meet up. The map only shows adventures posted today so you know they're happening.

'Cats Only

At this point, Jango is only being released to NU students.
